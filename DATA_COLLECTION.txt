AI ANALYSIS FRAMEWORK
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

All the above data is processed by Claude AI to generate:

1. Executive Summary (outcome prediction with confidence)
2. Tactical Analysis (formations, strategies, key battles)
3. Team Form & Momentum (recent performance trends)
4. League Position & Context (what's at stake)
5. Head-to-Head Insights (historical patterns)
6. Player Analysis & Key Battles (top performers, matchups)
7. Injury & Suspension Impact (critical absences)
8. Statistical Probabilities (goals, BTTS, clean sheets, etc.)
9. Goals by Time Period Analysis (scoring patterns)
10. Attacking Threat vs Defensive Solidity
11. Risk Assessment & Variables
12. Final Verdict & Recommendations
